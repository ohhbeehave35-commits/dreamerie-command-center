Privacy Notice

The Dreamerie / Suzy D · last updated July 2026

This chat assistant is provided by The Dreamerie / Suzy D. This page explains, in plain language, what happens with information you share with it.

What we collect

Only what you choose to tell the assistant in the chat — typically your name, phone number, and/or email address, and whatever you describe about what you need (e.g. a candle/gift order, a collab or affiliate inquiry). We don't collect anything about you before you start typing, and we don't require an account or login to chat.

Why we collect it

Solely to follow up with you about your request — to reply about an order, a collaboration, or a question. Nothing you share is used for advertising, and it is never sold to anyone.

Where it's stored

Information you share is stored in our business record-keeping system (Airtable), and your conversation may be reviewed by Susan or her team in order to follow up with you. It is not shared with any other company or used to train any public AI model.

How long we keep it

We keep it as long as reasonably needed to handle your request and maintain normal business records. If you'd like your information deleted sooner, just ask — see contact info below.

Your choices

  • You can chat without sharing any personal information — just don't volunteer it, and ask general questions instead.
  • You can ask, at any time, what information we have on file for you, or ask us to delete it.

Questions or requests

Contact us directly through the business (TikTok, email, or in person) and we'll take care of it.

This notice is a plain-language summary of our data practices, not a formal legal document.
